About Us

This is a story of two brothers Théodore & Achille Sauvage, coming from a tiny village called Rochefourchat situated in southeastern France, who despite all the obstacles and against all odds realised their dream of traveling the world and spreading laughter and happiness. In summer of 1928, the two brothers left their home village and started a long journey through France performing their clown-show and searching talented performers to join their troupe. By the end of the 5th month of their travels, they founded “Wild Circus” consisting of acrobats, dancers, singers, clowns, fire eaters, and jugglers. Today the circus is led by Sauvage brothers’ grandchildren and the troupe has given performances in almost every corner of the world.
